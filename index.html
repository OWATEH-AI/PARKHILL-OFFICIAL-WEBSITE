About Our College

Parkhill College is a disciplined and academically driven institution located in Domboshava Showgrounds, Zimbabwe. We are committed to academic excellence, leadership, and character development, serving families with a safe, structured, and values-driven learning environment.


  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Highly qualified and dedicated teachers
  • Focus on ZIMSEC curriculum with strong pass rates
  • Holistic development approach

Our Academic Excellence

ECD

Laying a strong foundation through early childhood development. Focus on cognitive, social, and emotional growth following Ministry of Education guidelines.

Primary (Grade 1-7)

Nurturing young minds with a core focus on Mathematics, Sciences, and Languages. We prepare students for Grade 7 ZIMSEC and Cambridge examinations with confidence.

Secondary (Form 1-6)

Advanced learning with specialized focus on academic results, leadership training, and digital literacy. Full ZIMSEC and Cambridge curriculum coverage for O and A Levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

School runs from 7:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday to Friday. Extramural activities are available after school until 4:30 PM.

Do you provide transport?

Yes, we offer school bus services to surrounding areas including Domboshava, Goromonzi, and Ruwa. Contact us for routes and fees.

What uniforms are required?

School uniforms can be purchased from our designated supplier. Navy blue for the college uniform with gold accents. Sports attire is required for PE classes.

Are meals provided?

Yes, we have a cafeteria serving nutritious meals. Students can also bring packed lunches. Breakfast is available from 7:00 AM.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

We offer sports (football, basketball, volleyball), music, drama, science club, debating society, and various cultural activities.

How do I track my child's progress?

We provide termly progress reports and parent-teacher meetings every term. You can also communicate with teachers via our administration office.
